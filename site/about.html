About Our Agrenes

Welcome...

Since 2014, at AGRENES, we have continuously contributed to the advancement of the agro-business sector in partnership with many companies and individuals.

The services provided by AGRENES are based on the understanding that agricultural development and environmental management are interdependent and as such, technologies and innovations generated for agricultural development need an environmental lens to ensure sustainability.

Our official story start in 2014s

AGRENES is a private, for-profit entity in Uganda registered in 2014. AGRENES works with multi-national agencies, international development partners, and local and national institutions, including universities, research organizations, and other government departments, to deliver lasting solutions in sustainable agriculture, ecosystems, and environmental management.

AGRENES strives to support local farmers boost incomes, food, and nutritional security; for the improved livelihood of societies in Uganda. At AGRENES, we also respond to the indigenous communities’ development needs, targeting Uganda’s most vulnerable and resource-constrained societies.

We also build the capacity of local communities and institutions in sustainable agriculture mainly livestock production, crop husbandry, agroforestry, water for production, climate change mitigation, and adaptation practices.

Our Objective

To work with farmers to maximize their agriculture productivity, incurring low costs and generating more profits, encourage climate-smart practices.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable development and improve the livelihood of local farming communities by introducing and scaling contextually relevant, research-proven, evidence-based agricultural innovations and technology transfer.

Our Vision

Working with farmers to maximize their productivity in agriculture, incurring low costs and generating more profits, encourage climate-smart practices.
